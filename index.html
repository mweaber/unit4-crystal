Welcome to the Crystal Game

Welcome to The Crystal Game! The object of the game is to match the randomly generated score by clicking on the crystal below. Click the button to begin the game and with each round the crystals below will be worth various amounts. Click to update your score and try to match the given amount. If you go over the amount the game is over and you'll have to start again. Have fun!
